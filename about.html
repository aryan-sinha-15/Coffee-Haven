About Coffee Haven

At Coffee Haven, every sip tells a story. We're not just a coffee shop — we're a destination for coffee lovers. Founded with a mission to serve premium quality and create moments that matter, our space is as warm as our brew.

Our beans come from ethically sourced farms in Colombia, Brazil, and Ethiopia. Each roast is handcrafted by experts to unlock flavors that linger long after the cup is empty.

Our Mission

To deliver authentic coffee experiences while supporting ethical sourcing and community connection.

Our Vision

To become a global symbol of sustainable and passionate coffee culture.

Meet the Founders

Discover our coffee-obsessed journey.

Our founders, Maya and Arjun, started Coffee Haven in a cozy garage with a second-hand espresso machine and a dream to make every brew meaningful. Today, it’s a community movement.
